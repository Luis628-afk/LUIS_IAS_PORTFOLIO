CYBER SECURITY WRITE APP

Phishing Email Detector Practice

A concept app that trains users to spot red flags in suspicious emails.

Step 1

Define the Learning Goal

I want this app to help users practice telling the difference between a real email and a phishing attack.

  • Show short, realistic email examples
  • Let the user choose Phishing or Legit
  • Give clear explanations after each choice
Step 2

Design the Email View

Each question in the app looks like a mini inbox item or email preview so it feels familiar:

From: security@bank‑verify.com
Subject: Urgent: Verify your account now
Dear customer, your account will be locked in 24 hours if you do not click the link below and confirm your information.
Step 3

Create the Quiz Logic

Behind each email, I define why it is phishing or safe:

  • Suspicious email address or domain
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Links that do not match the official website
  • Spelling or grammar mistakes
Step 4

Explain the Answer

After the user answers, the app shows a short explanation:

  • Highlights the exact red flags in the email
  • Explains what a safe version would look like
  • Teaches the user to pause and think before clicking
Result

Why This Matters

Many real attacks start with phishing. This write app shows how UI, simple logic, and explanations can help people protect themselves.
